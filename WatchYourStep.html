Watch Your Step Synopsis

Watch your Step was the first ever game jam entry I made. Made for the Brackey's Game Jam around the theme of 'It Is Not Real'. Made with a friend, the game is designed around the genre of rage platformers where the player must climb to the top of the map to win. However, some platforms may not be real as the player must figure out where to jump and where to avoid.

Some features this game has:

  • Rage-Platformer style movement
  • Coins in the map to test your platformer skills
  • Timer to test your speed-run capabilities
