Grade: ??%

Crop Quest was my final major project for my level 6 degree in game design and production. As a team of 4 we were tasked to build the game we spent 3 months previous planning using industry standard pre-production techniques like prototyping, greenlight process and market analysis.

The game features a host of cozy farming simulator features:

  • A host of crops to plant and farm.
  • Visitors to trade your hard farmed crops with.
  • Expandable island plots and upgrades to grow your farm bigger.
  • Critters that will eat your crops unless you catch them.
