In My Next Life Synopsis

In My Next Life is a small game about experiencing a new life as animals! run and fly through the small minigames getting as many points as you can! Made in a week for the Brackey's Game Jam, it was made around the theme of "An End is a New Beginning", the player goes through the life of a character experiencing reincarnation.

Some features this game has:

  • Minigame style games
  • popular running genre minigame
  • flappy bird style minigame
