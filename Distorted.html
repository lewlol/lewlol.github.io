Distorted Synopsis

Distorted was a solo game jam entry to the Mini Jam under the theme of colours. The game is a platformer designed around running from your evil self who follows your every movement. Platform your way through spikes, damaged floors, and ascender blocks using your kit of running jumping and walljumping!

Some features this game has:

  • speedy paced platformer
  • spikes, damaged blocks, and ascender blocks to spice up the platforming
  • hidden cherries as collectables.
