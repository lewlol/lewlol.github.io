Isolation Synopsis

Isolation was a game jam entry for the Brackey's Game Jam around the theme of "You Are Not Alone". In this game you explore the Universe as a lost spaceship trying to make it back to earth. Just when you feel alone in the vastness of space you encounter alien species that are interested in different things. Some friendly, some foe, and some are just wanting to trade for your gold nuggets! Manage your fuel count by collecting resources and destroying planets to make it home before you lose it all!

Some features this game has:

  • vast world to explore
  • 4 types of alien species with different AI
  • fuel management system and gold nugget currency
