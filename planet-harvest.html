Dale Synopsis

Grade: DISTINCTION

Dale was our interpretation of a mining simulator based on the theme of 'Diving Deeper'. We tried to keep a time based progression scheme with the day system with some limitations like stamina. The player must buy upgrades to get further down as the ores and gems get better. Eventually the player will reach the end room with lots of treasure.

Some features this game has:

  • Surplus of ores, gems, items, and loot.
  • Day system to sell gained ores and gems.
  • Upgrade Shop to use gained money on.
  • Procedurally generated world every time the player sleeps.
